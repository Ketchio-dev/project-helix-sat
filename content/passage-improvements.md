# Reading and Writing Passage Improvements

## `rw_words_context_01`

- Current passage (149 chars):

> Researchers described the new coral-growth model as elegant because it captured seasonal variation without relying on dozens of unstable assumptions.

- Improved passage:

> Marine ecologists described the new coral-growth model as elegant not because it was elaborate, but because it was disciplined and efficient. By capturing seasonal variation through a small set of well-supported variables, the model reproduced observed reef patterns without depending on the dozens of unstable assumptions that had weakened earlier simulations.

- Choice text adjustments:
None recommended. The expanded passage still supports `B. streamlined` as the most precise choice.

## `rw_transition_01`

- Current passage (199 chars):

> The neighborhood museum extended its hours on Friday evenings. Attendance from local families rose during the first month. ______, the museum decided to keep the expanded schedule through the summer.

- Improved passage:

> After the neighborhood museum extended its hours on Friday evenings, staff members observed a noticeable increase in attendance from local families, especially those who had previously struggled to visit during the workweek. Because that rise continued beyond the first month and improved turnout for public programs, ______, the museum decided to keep the expanded schedule through the summer.

- Choice text adjustments:
None recommended. The added detail strengthens the causal link, so `A. As a result,` remains the best transition.

## `rw_revision_01`

> The robotics club built a small underwater drone for the regional showcase, ______ the team tested it in a nearby pool before the event.

> Over several weeks, the robotics club built a small underwater drone for the regional showcase, carefully sealing the electronics, balancing the propellers, and testing the camera mount for leaks, ______ the team tested it in a nearby pool before the event to confirm that it could steer, surface, and record footage reliably under realistic conditions.

- Choice text adjustments:
None recommended. The sentence still requires a coordinating conjunction, so `B. and the team tested it in a nearby pool before the event.` remains correct.

## `rw_words_context_02`

> Over decades of fieldwork, Professor Irani cultivated relationships with village councils across the region, learning to navigate local protocols before requesting access to archaeological sites.

None recommended. The expanded wording makes `B. deliberately developed` even more clearly correct without changing the skill target.
